Campus Photographic History Book Available for Purchase

The University of Idaho Library is excited to announce the publication of a new book capturing the history of the University of Idaho. Written by Professor of History Katherine G. Aiken and Head of Special Collections and Archives Erin Passehl Stoddart, this pictorial volume features over 200 photographs from UI Library Special Collections and Archives. The book also features a foreword written by UI President Chuck Staben.

The scope of the book encompasses both the long history of the university and new images from the last thirty years. Chapters focus on buildings on the Moscow campus, regional centers, and extension offices; academic departments and degrees; Vandal athletics; student life; and university organizations. The book builds on two previously published histories of the University of Idaho: The Beacon of Mountain and Plain by Rafe Gibbs and This Crested Hill by former state historian Keith Petersen.

Kathy Aiken, Professor of History

Erin Stoddart, Head of Special Collections

The book may be purchased starting September 19, 2016 through the University of Idaho Library at the Circulation Desk on the First Floor for $21.99 plus tax using cash, check, or credit card. Books can also be mailed with a shipping fee, please contact Jennifer Mylan (jmylan@uidaho.edu) or Sammantha Green (samm@uidaho.edu) to place an order.
