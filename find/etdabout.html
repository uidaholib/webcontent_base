Theses and Dissertations Database

As of January 2014, all theses and dissertations are submitted electronically to the College of Graduate Studies. Electronic theses and dissertations are also, with student permission, uploaded to the Library's institutional repository after graduation. In addition to the digital version, which will be displayed in the database and online here, an archival paper copy is held in Special Collections & Archives. For more information on the College of Graduate Studies' submission process contact Melinda Deyasi and for more information on the digital preservation of theses and dissertations contact Annie Gaines.

The theses and dissertations database is updated yearly.
