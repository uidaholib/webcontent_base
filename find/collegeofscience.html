Directions:

  • 1. Find a DVD (or DVDs) using the search, drop-down, or button features.
  • 2. Click on the Title of the DVD(s) you find to see if the DVD(s) is available.
  • 3. If the DVD is available, write down the Call Number and take it to the circulation desk.
  • 4. Circulation will check out your DVD using your Vandal Card.
