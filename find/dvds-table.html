Directions:

  • 1. Find a DVD (or DVDs) using the search options below. You can search by title, date, lanuage, cast member, and other fields.
  • 2. Click on the Title of the DVD(s) you find to see if the DVD(s) is available.
  • 3. If the DVD is available, copy down the Call Number and go to the DVDs area on the second floor to find your DVD.
  • 4. Take the DVD container to Circulation on the first floor. They will check out the DVD to you using your Vandal Card.
