Library Instruction and Tours

Tours for new students and members of campus were scheduled at the beginning of the academic year.

However, we are always happy to provide tours by appointment. If you would like a tour, contact Diane Prorak at prorak@uidaho.edu.

If you need additional help finding information in the library, ask at the Reference Desk on the 1st floor, 885-6584, libref@uidaho.edu or, for more in-depth research help, make a research appointment through:

  • Faculty Research Assistance Program
