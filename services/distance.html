Distance Education

The resources of University of Idaho Library are available for students both in and outside of Moscow, Idaho. If you are taking classes online or at one of the University of Idaho centers, check out our research guide.

The guide and information below will also be useful for students taking UI classes online, or even those from Moscow who are using the library's resources while traveling or from home.
