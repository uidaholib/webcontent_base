UI Vision for Data Management

The University of Idaho Library strives to help researchers at the University of Idaho become familiar with data management practices. Library faculty will endeavor to communicate information about data storage standards and protocols, metadata reporting requirements and best practices, and the requirements and recommendations of national funding bodies and/or professional associations. The library will also serve as a resource for intellectual property issues arising from the creation of data.
