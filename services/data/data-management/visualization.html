Data Visualization

Data visualization is the visual representation of data. Graphical depictions of data may help communicate information more clearly and effectively. Therefore, researchers may be interested in exploring ways to visualize their data. There are a number of data visualization software programs in existence and the University of Idaho has an IQ Station for true 3-D immersive data visualization that researchers may be interested in using.
