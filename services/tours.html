Library Tours

Tours for new students and other members of the UI community are held at the beginning of each academic year. We are also happy to provide tours by appointment. If you would like a tour, contact Diane Prorak at prorak@uidaho.edu.

If you need additional help finding information in the library, ask at the Reference Desk on the 1st floor, email us at libref@uidaho.edu, or call at 885-6584.
