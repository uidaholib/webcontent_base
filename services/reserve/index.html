Student Driven Reserves Pilot

As a part of a pilot program, the library is now accepting requests from students to place books on reserve. To request materials, fill out this form:

Student Reserves Request Form.

Requested books must be designated as required in the course syllabus. Requested titles may not include supplemental readings or books recommended for course research projects or further reading. For more information about this pilot, contact Evan Williamson.
