General Information

Over the next several months, the University of Idaho Library will be revamping its first floor to provide patrons with improved study areas, expanded classroom resources, and a new coffee and refreshment installation. During the construction process the location of library services and holdings may change, but will remain available to patrons with a minimum of inconvenience.

Please visit our First Floor Renovation Guide for more information on specific anticipated changes, or feel free ask our circulation staff or reference librarians any questions you may have.

Timeline

Although you may have noticed changes to the first floor throughout the spring 2015 semester, the project is officially planned to begin summer of 2015. Certain portions of the project will be phased in gradually; for instance, the circulation desk will not be moved to its temporary location on the second floor until just prior to the start of the fall term.

Construction is planned to last through February of 2016, although this date may change according to need.
